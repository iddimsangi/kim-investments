    About Us

    KIM Investments Company Limited is a Tanzania based registered Construction Company, with certificate of Incorporation No.xxx dated 20th May 2012 and also registered with Contractors Registration Board (CRB) class X in building with registration No. B5/05XX/0X/20XX as well as class six in civil works with registration No. CXC/0NNN/01/05.

    Our Mission

    To perform for our customers the highest level of quality construction services at fair and market competitive prices and also to maintain the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty and fairness in our relationships with our suppliers, subcontractors, professional associates and customers.

    Our Plan

    To be the preeminent provider of superior construction services by consistently improving the quality of our product and to add value for clients through innovation, foresight, integrity, and aggressive performance; and to serve with character and purpose that brings honor to God.

    Our Vision

    KIM Investments’s vision is to be the leading construction company in our chosen markets, sought after by potential clients and employees for our track record in reliable execution, cost effectiveness, and world class technical sophistication.

    Services

    KIM Investments company specializes in customizing its approach to the needs and challenges of each project, no matter the size and scope.
